Victoria Sponge Cake

Main Cake

ingredients:
  • 200g caster sugar
  • 200g unsalted butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 200g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbs milk
How to smush it all together

Pre heat the oven to 190 on fan
line two tins with parchment paper
Then in a large bowl, mix the sugar, butter, eggs, flour, baking powder and milk till until smooth (To adjust the consdistancy add more or less milk)
Divide the mixture into the two bowls use a spoon to spread the mixture till even
Bake for 20 mins until gloden and cooked in the inside(use a tooth pick to check)
Put them on a cooling rack and resist the urge to eat it (I know it's hard)

Icing and filling

ingredients
  • 100g softened butter
  • 150g icing sugar
  • vanilla extract
    (Or if you are fancy, you can use vanilla beans)
  • 350g strawberry jam
How to smush it all together

Use a spatula to mix the butter until it is smooth
Gradually mix in the icing sugar to the butter
Add in a drop of vanilla extract
Decorating:
Spread the buttercream over the bottom of one of the sponges
top it with the jam then put the last cake layer on
Dust with icing sugar and decorate with cream and raspberries to be fancy and enjoy :)
