Chocolate lava cake

ingredients
  • 250g chocolate
  • 50g butter
  • 100g power sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 50g flower

How to smush it all together

Melt a bit of butter than brush it onto the a silicon mold
Preheat the oven to 200 degrees
In a plastic bowl melt the chocolate and butter at 500 w for two minutes
Add the power sugar, eggs, flower to the melted chocolate and whisk together till combined
Cook for 11 minutes
let it set (Which takes around 10-15 minutes) then take it out of the mold carefully.
And enjoy!
